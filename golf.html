Twin Oaks golf course is a golf course in Northern Kentucky. It was established in 1977. The course features 18 holes totalling 6392 yards of golf.

It costs you $48 to play 18 holes of golf riding on a golf cart. If you want to play, call the course at (859)581-2410.

Aston Oaks golf club is a Jack Nicklaus designed course in Cincinnati, Ohio. This course is very beautiful and features holes overlooking the Ohio River. The course totals 7018 yards for 18 holes.

A round of golf here costs $32 for 18 holes. To contact them, call (513)467-0070.

Devou golf park is a course in Northern Kentucky. The front 9 opened in 1922 and the last 9 of the course opened in 1995. The course totals 6091 yards in 18 holes.

This course costs $50 for 18 holes. Their phone number is (859)431-8030
