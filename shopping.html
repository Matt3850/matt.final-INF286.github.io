Shopping:

Kenwood Shopping center is a mall located in Cincinnati, Ohio. The mall features over 180 shops and shopping here is a great way to spend a day in Cincinnati.

Kenwood opens at 10 am on weekdays and 11 am on sundays. Their phone number is (513)745-9100.

The Florence Mall is a mall located in Northern Kentucky. It was opened in 1976. The mall features over 100 stores and has a food court.

The mall is open from 11-8 on weekdays and 12-6 on Sunday.
